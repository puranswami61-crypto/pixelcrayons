How quickly can your white-label teams start on my client project?

We can onboard your dedicated pod within 72 hours. Each pod includes developers, marketers, or analysts matched to your agency’s current workflow and tech stack.

Yes, every engagement is NDA-backed and executed 100% under your agency brand. We never pitch or communicate directly with your clients.

Do we get to choose the talent or team assigned?

Absolutely. You’ll review vetted profiles before finalizing your pod. Once approved, your team stays consistent – ensuring delivery predictability.

Every project follows QA-bound, SLA-driven processes, with proof-of-work tracking, milestone visibility, and feedback loops built into every sprint.

How do you handle communication and reporting?

Your team collaborates through your preferred tools – Slack, ClickUp, or Jira – with transparent reporting via weekly check-ins and dashboards.

What’s included in the “fit call”?

It’s a 15-minute discovery call to align your goals, project types, and client needs. You’ll get a custom pod recommendation, estimated cost, and timeline.

How is billing and IP handled?

Billing is monthly or milestone-based, with 100% IP ownership staying with your agency or client – never with us.
